Features

Nutrient Automation

Our system intelligently analyzes plant needs and automatically adjusts nutrient levels, ensuring optimal growth conditions.

NutriBuddy's AI provides real-time insights into plant health, growth trends, and possible issues, enabling proactive decision-making.

Monitor and control your hydroponic setup remotely through our user-friendly mobile app, giving you peace of mind wherever you are.

Stay informed and receive alerts on your mobile device regarding changes in nutrient levels, water temperature, and other crucial factors. Monitor any issues promptly, even when you're on the go.
